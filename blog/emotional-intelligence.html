Emotional Intelligence.

What is Emotional Intelligence?

EQ is the ability to understand, manage and express your own emotions while also recognizing and responding to the emotions of other people around you.


How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is different to IQ because it refers to the emotional abilities + social skills where as IQ refers to cognitive abilities like problem solving or analysing.


Why is EQ important?

Why is EQ important? EQ is important because it allows people to understand and manage their own emotions as well as others emotions which improve relationships, decision making and makes for the ideal leadership candidate.
